==== Erin's Law Lessons ====
{{*erinslaw.png}}
Three times per year Oregon law specifies that we teach developmentally appropriate sexual abuse prevention. We will begin this instruction during the week of December 11th. Each grade offers instruction that is developmentally appropriate and designed to protect our students. If you have questions about the curriculum being used please reach out to your classroom teacher.
