Electronics Waste Roundup

For the 2nd year in a row Holt will be participating in a collaborative effort to reduce electronics waste in our community. We want your electronics! Do you have old computers, toasters, alarm clocks or other items taking up space at home or work? We want it!

Holt is competing in a competition against other schools in 4j. The school that collects the most electronics waste will win. Any unwanted items can be brought to Holt during the first week of May. All items collected will be take to NextStep Recycling.
