HOLTOBERFEST is this week!

Mark your calendar to join family and friends this Friday, 10/28 from 6-8 pm at the school.

Avoid lines and get right to the fun by purchasing your tickets in advance. Volunteers will be at the curb during pick up this week starting on Tuesday to take pre-orders. Forms will also come home this week in the Monday mailer. As an added bonus, pre-ordered tickets will be hand delivered to students in a special fall themed bag perfect for collecting prizes during Holtoberfest! Check the Monday Mailer flyer for prices.

Do not forget the costume guidelines for all school events:

  • No masks or face coverings that make it difficult to identify who you are
  • No toy or real weapons of any kind
  • No costumes making fun of individuals, cultures, or ethnicities
