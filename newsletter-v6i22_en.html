Signing In

Please remember that all families wishing to join their student at breakfast must sign in at the front office. Please also sign out. We have an electronic badge reader in the front office. You simply need to scan your badge to sign out each day. It's important to us that we know who is in the building at all times. Please help us increase security and safety by signing in and out when you visit Holt.

Did You Know

Each class at Holt has a buddy classroom. Holt uses the Caring Schools Community Curriculum and Program Materials to create meaningful experiences four students of varying ages. We connect younger students with an older, role model in a classroom in the 3-5 wing. The students have fun engaging in a monthly experience or activity. Please check out the buddy art and activities that are displayed throughout our school.
