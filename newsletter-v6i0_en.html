Volume 6, Issue 1 September 1st, 2016

Meet the Teacher Day

In this optional event you can come find your student's desk, meet their teacher, pay your school supply fee, and have fun doing it!

From 2:30-3:30 Tuesday, September 6th. Enter through the front doors.

Kindergarten Students Have Staggered Start

Students with the last name beginning with A-L begin on Wednesday, September 7th. They do not attend on Thursday, September 8th.

Kindergarten students with the last name beginning with M-Z begin school on Thursday, September 8th.

All kindergarten students attend together on Friday, September 9th.

  • Sept 6: Meet the Teacher 2:30-3:30
  • Sept 7: First Day of School Grades 1-5
  • Sept 7: First Day of School for Kinders with last names A-L Only
  • Sept 8: First Day of School for Kinders with last names M-Z Only
  • Sept 8: No School for Kinders with last names A-L Only
  • Sept 9: All Kinders together at school

Remember Our Earlier Start Time This Year

Just a reminder, school starts at 7:55am this year. Students need to be in their seats at 7:55.

Pay Your School Supply Fee

Holt purchases all school supplies for your students. We charge $25 per year. This covers both supplies and field trips. Please see the office to pay your fees, or send a check in with your student in their Monday mailer. Make checks payable to Bertha Holt Elementary.
