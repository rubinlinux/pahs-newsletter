Holiday Science

The Science Factory has opportunities for hands-on learning during the holidays! Kids in grades 1-3 can sign up for our Holiday Science Adventures during the week of December 19-23. Enjoy half-day workshops on robotics, toy building, penguins, holiday treats, and more. We include lots of time for exploring our hands-on exhibits as well. See the complete list of workshops and download a registration form at http://sciencefactory.org/workshops.
