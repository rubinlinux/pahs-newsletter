Holt Bolt

It has been one week since the Holt Bolt and we have collected $5000. We are 1/7th of the way to our school goal of $35,000. If we get to our first school-wide prize tier of $10,000, we will have a private evening at Skateworld for just our students and their families, so please continue to bring in those donations!
