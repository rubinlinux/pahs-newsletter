School Days Restored by Calendar Changes

The Eugene School District has adjusted this year's school calendar to restore five of the school days that were missed due to hazardous winter weather. Four school days will be made up at the end of the school year in June. The new final day for students is Thursday, June 22.

At elementary schools, the fifth school day will be restored on May 5, which was previously scheduled to be a no-school day for teacher planning and professional development. Certain other days also have been changed in the revised calendar to balance the school year.

Vaccine Exclusion Day is Coming!

Students must have current school records of all required vaccinations or valid exemptions to continue attending school on and after Exclusion Day, Feb. 17, 2016. If you have questions about which shots are required, please visit the 4j page (http://www.4j.lane.edu/instruction/ess/health/immunization/) or call Nurse Cathy at 541-790-6110.

Please Remember to Sign-in at the Front Office Each Time You Enter

Please remember that all adults and non-Holt students need to sign in each time they enter the school. This includes coming into the cafeteria in the morning. Families should enter through the front doors, sign in, and then head down to the cafeteria. Please do not enter through the gym/cafeteria doors with your students.
