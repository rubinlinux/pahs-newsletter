Clean and Healthy Teeth

February is Children's Oral Health Month! Watch for free Dental Kits coming home today. Kits contain simple, preventative measures for healthy teeth. These kits provide a great opportunity to talk with your student about brushing, flossing, and oral health.

Parking Lot Reminder

Please remember that you must make a right turn when exiting the main school lot. Our right turn only sign fell down earlier this year and was recently remounted. Please also remember that when dropping off in front of the school the internal lane does not stop. Please do not attempt to pick up or drop off your students if you can't safely pull to the curb. You can always loop the lot until a space is available.

Want to ease parking lot congestion and help students increase their activity by walking and biking to school safely?

Let's make walking and rolling the way to go! The 4J Safe Routes to School program is looking for one or more parents at Bertha Holt to work with us as a liaison for Walk + Bike to School day in May and, if there's interest, help with other related activities. Contact Sarah Mazze, 4J Safe Routes to School Coordinator, at mazze_s@4j.lane.edu or 541-516-0887 to get involved or to share concerns about barriers to active transportation for the Holt community and ideas for solutions.
