Holt Bolt

This friday is our Jog-A-Thon, the Holt Bolt! Please join us for a morning of running. All students need to be dressed for excercise. We run rain or shine. We will pass out our Holt Bolt shirts on the day of the event. Families are welcome to join us. Please sign in at the front office if you are attending this fun event.

Bolt Times:

  • 10:00-11am - 1st, 2nd, 1/2 Blend, & 3rd grades
