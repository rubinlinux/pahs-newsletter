Holt Bolt a Huge Success!

Our annual Holt Bolt was a tremendous success! Thank you to all families and volunteers for an amazing event. The kids had a blast and really enjoyed their shirts. We are thankful to our generous sponsors who provided these for our students. All money and envelopes are due May 12, 2016. Parents/Guardians must sign the envelopes for their students. This year the prize structure is a bit different. We will earn different prizes as a school based on how much we raise. Our goal is $35,000. If we hit that we will have a pool party at Amazon Pool for just our families. We will also have some raffles for any student who turns in a signed envelope. No fund-raising is required to be entered.

The Holt Bolt is our only school fund-raiser. We truly appreciate everyone's efforts to raise money for our school!
