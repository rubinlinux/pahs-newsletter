==== Site Council ====
The Holt Site Council is an elected committee of staff and parents, primarily
focused on school improvement. Our meetings are open to the public, and we
welcome parents, staff, and community members.

At Site council this week, we will be sharing the 2016-2017 School Report
Card.  This Thursday, Nov 16th in the Library from 5-6:30.

==== PAHS Board ====
PAHS is our Holt "Parents At Holt School" parent board. We organize
activities, work on fundraisers, and generally keep up to date with things at
Holt. 

We welcomed several new faces at our last meeting! Please join us again, or come fir the first time: 
Thursday, Nov 16th in the Library from 6:30-8pm right after Site Council.
