School Choice Lottery & Showcase

The School Choice process has now started for all in-district families. If you moved out of Holt's boundaries this year, or will move this year, the only way to continue next year is to enter the lottery.

Lottery

To be included in the annual lottery for the 2017–18 school year, 4J residents must apply between Tuesday, January 3 and Tuesday, February 28 at 5 p.m. Non-district residents must apply between Wednesday, March 1 and Saturday, April 1 at 5 p.m.

Please know that Holt took very few students off of the lottery this year. We are running near capacity and don't anticipate a decrease in enrollment in the near future. Current students are prioritized within the lottery process.

Weather Closure Make-up

The 2016–17 school calendar includes weather closure make-up days in June, and the ice storms mean they’ll be put to use. 4J schools were closed due to hazardous weather for five days in December & January so far. The missed days will be made up at the end of the school year.

The last day of school for students will be Wednesday, June 23, 2017, unless there are more school closure days due to weather. If there are more school closure days, additional days will be made up and the final day will be later.
