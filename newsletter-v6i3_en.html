Morning Drop-Off Etiquette

Please observe the following rules to help us ensure a safe morning drop-off:

  • Be extremely alert when navigating the parking lot and surrounding streets. Kids are small, and can be quick and unpredictable.
  • Don't leave your vehicle in the drop-off lane between 7:30-8:10am
  • Absolutely no parking in the reserved disabled spots without a disabled permit. It is never allowed to park in the striped access spots adjacent to them.
  • North Park Community Church (Across Harlow Rd.) has offered us use of their parking lot, in case Holt's is full.

After-School Art Class: Sports, Art, and Product Design

The Jordan Schitzer Art Museum is hosting a class on Wednesdays, October 5th to December 7th at 3:30. (Grades 1-5)

Inspired by the exhibition Scrimmage: Football in American Art from the Civil War to the Present and contemporary artists Catherine Opie and Shaun Leonardo, students will explore how images can illustrate significant events. Using Copic markers and mixed-media, students will create their own sports-related products, such as running shoes and helmets. Register Here

Scholarships are available for financial support.
